About the Project

Hey, Newcomers!

Artyom, Amalie, and Raluca hope you stay warm and cozy this Estonian winter.

We collected some frequently asked questions (FAQs) on the subject and reached out to leading experts in the field to try to shed light on the issue. Read on for more helpful hints!

Surviver Expert Suggests

Hello, my name is Leo, and I've lived through 38 Estonian winters. I understand that the Estonian winter may seem overwhelming to you, especially if you are used to living in warmer climates.
